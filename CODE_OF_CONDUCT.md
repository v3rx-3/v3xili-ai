# Code of Conduct

V3XILI AI welcomes useful, respectful and specific feedback.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Explain problems clearly.
- Share screenshots, steps and context when reporting issues.
- Keep discussion focused on the product, roadmap and user experience.

## Not accepted

- Harassment, insults or personal attacks
- Spam or low-quality promotion
- Publicly sharing sensitive vulnerabilities before responsible disclosure
- Attempts to abuse, bypass or damage the service

## Enforcement

Issues or comments that are spammy, abusive, unsafe or off-topic may be closed, hidden or removed.
