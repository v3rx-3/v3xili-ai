# Contributing to V3XILI AI

Thanks for your interest in V3XILI AI.

This public repository is mainly used for product updates, feedback, roadmap visibility and community discussion. The production codebase may not be fully open source.

## Best ways to contribute

- Open a feedback issue with your use case.
- Suggest a feature that would improve study, coding, research or creation workflows.
- Report UX problems with screenshots and device/browser details.
- Share integration ideas for Plugging, MCP-style tools or developer workflows.
- Suggest improvements for kazaAI, the upcoming development-agent direction.

## Feedback format

Good feedback usually includes:

1. What you were trying to do.
2. What worked well.
3. What felt confusing or slow.
4. What feature would make you use it more.
5. Your device/browser if the issue is visual or technical.

## Product principles

V3XILI AI prioritizes:

- Privacy-first AI workflows
- Clean desktop and mobile UX
- Practical tools over hype
- Study, coding, research and creation use cases
- Safe integrations and clear user control
- Long-term maintainability
